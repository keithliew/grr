The Programme

How the 15-Year Rental Programme Works

Years 1–3Fixed Initial Phase

Year 1Year 3Year 15

Years 1–3 — Fixed Initial Phase

  • Rental at 8% per annum under the tenancy agreement
  • Payable quarterly in arrear
  • Developer covers outgoings — maintenance charges and sinking fund — for the first 3 years
  • Option: rental in advance at 6.5% p.a. (nett) during construction

Years 4–15 — Profit-Sharing Phase

  • Rental on a profit-sharing basis, paid monthly
  • 75% of nett operating profit apportioned among participating owners
  • 25% shared between developer and operator
  • 10 complimentary nights' stay per year
  • Fully managed — furnishing, upkeep and hotel operations handled by the operator
  • Exit the tenancy with 3 months' written notice
